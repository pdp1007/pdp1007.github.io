Before: Browser Extension After: Native Edge Integration

From flatlined deployments to runaway adoption

For several months, signal capture through the browser extension delivered only modest results — despite deployment attempts from thousands of tenant admins, weekly active users remained limited and WW engagement stayed modest through last month. Following the rollout of the native Edge integration, both tenant and user metrics have shown a significant increase, strengthening the foundation that improves personalization across Copilot Chat, Search & WorkIQ.
